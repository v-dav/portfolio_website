Welcome

My name is Vladimir Davidov and I'm 33 years old. I'm a junior web developer finishing my specialization at Holberton School France and currently moving to Sweden. As additional education I have a doctorate in Medicine from the University of Toulouse.


I've always been passionate about computer science, and after 15 years of priceless and invaluable experience studying and practicing medicine, the time has finally come to fullfill my deepest dreams. Work with computers and data and explore their endless possibilities.


It all started with old gold C. It was tough but I loved it. My background has equipped my with resilience, learning and problem solving skills and helped me to start this new journey. I'm not (yet) an expert in algorithms and data structures but I recognize the subtle beauty of the logic behind. I enjoy to build the back-end of an app and my creative and visual myself is also attracted by the front-end part.


I'm a quiet person, naturally curious and perpetually working on improving my chops. When I'm not behind the computer I'm usually rock climbing, reading books or hanging out with my partner and cat.
